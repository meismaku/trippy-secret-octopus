  • Tesla: Find the Spark

    Insight: Market a car brand in a way that's never been done before

    Find the Spark is a campaign to broadcast the Tesla Motors brand in a way that has never been done before. One of the major concerns with electric vehicles is the ability to charge these vehicles on the road and away from home. Through a "real life" scavenger hunt, app users locate different parking locations that have a plug outlet or electric vehicle charger nearby. Each tag will enter users into a drawing to win a Tesla electric vehicle.
